About KCVA

KCVA News Channel 6 is a non-profit independent television station for northern San Diego run entirely by teens. Our Station was founded September 24, 2023 and has broadcast over the air on VHF 6 ever since, and have begun to broadcast over a website. We make all are graphics from scratch and our newscasts are done live in real time. To watch our reports click the "Watch" Tab.
